2026 | FIR Lab will join HKISI-CAS in June 2026

FIR Lab will join the Hong Kong Institute of Science & Innovation, Chinese Academy of Sciences, in June 2026. Professor Zhang will serve as an independent PI at the Centre for Artificial Intelligence and Robotics (CAIR).

2026 | Website updated to a fully static academic group format

The lab website has been reorganized into a conventional static academic structure with research, people, publications, news, events, and openings pages.

2026 | VLAbot published in Robotics and Computer-Integrated Manufacturing

Recent work explores human vision-language-action interaction frameworks for robotic assembly.

2025 | Professor Zhang joined HKUST(GZ) in September 2025

After promotion to Associate Professor with tenure, Professor Zhang officially joined The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou).

2025 | RoPESim accepted to IEEE CASE 2025

The project provides a framework for evaluating robot manipulation policies through simulation.

2024 | RADAR presented at IEEE/RSJ IROS 2024

The system demonstrates robotics assembly by demonstration via augmented reality.

2024 | AR teleoperation work on self-supervised 6-DoF grasping released
