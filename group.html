People

Yunbo "Will" Zhang

Associate Professor with tenure
Thrust of Intelligent Manufacturing, The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou)

Dr. Yunbo "Will" Zhang is currently Associate Professor with tenure at HKUST(GZ). He received his Ph.D. from The Chinese University of Hong Kong in 2012 and conducted postdoctoral research at Purdue University from 2014 to 2017.

He joined Rochester Institute of Technology in 2019 as Assistant Professor, was promoted to Associate Professor with tenure in 2025, and officially joined HKUST(GZ) in September 2025.

His research focuses on human-robot collaboration, robot learning, manufacturing AI, and AR/VR-enabled intelligent interaction. His recognitions include the 2024 ASME CIE Technical Committee Leadership Award and the Silver Medal at the 2019 Geneva International Exhibition of Inventions.

He also serves as Associate Editor for journals including Frontiers in Robotics and AI and the Journal of Intelligent Manufacturing.

In June 2026, Professor Zhang will join the Hong Kong Institute of Science & Innovation, Chinese Academy of Sciences, where he will serve as an independent PI at the Centre for Artificial Intelligence and Robotics (CAIR).
